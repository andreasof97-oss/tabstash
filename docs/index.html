Features

Everything you need to tame your tabs

Powerful enough to replace tab hoarding. Simple enough to use in one click.

💾

One-Click Save

Save all open tabs as a named session instantly. Give it a name, pick a color, done. Your tabs are safe even if Chrome crashes.

🎨

Color-Coded Sessions

Choose from 8 vibrant colors to visually organize your sessions. Work in blue, personal in green, research in purple — you decide.

🔄

Smart Restore

Restore sessions in a new window or merge into your current one. Pick the mode that fits your workflow.

✋

Drag & Drop Reorder

Rearrange sessions by dragging them into the order you want. Keep your most important sessions at the top.

🔍

Quick Search

Search across all sessions and URLs instantly. Find that one tab you saved last week in seconds, not minutes.

🚫

Duplicate Detection

TabStash spots duplicate URLs when you save and lets you skip them. No more saving the same page five times.

📥

Import from OneTab

Switching from OneTab? Import all your saved tabs with one click. Zero data loss, zero hassle.

🔢

Tab Count Badge

See how many tabs you have saved right on the extension icon. A subtle reminder that your tabs are safely stashed.

v1.1📁

Session Folders

Group related sessions into folders. Keep work, personal, and projects neatly organized. Coming in v1.1.

⌨️

Keyboard Shortcuts

Press Ctrl+Shift+Y to open TabStash instantly. Save and restore without ever touching your mouse.
